BMI Calculator

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a calculation that estimates body fat based on height and weight. It's used to categorize individuals into weight categories like underweight, normal weight, overweight, and obese. The formula is BMI = kg/m², where kg is weight in kilograms and m² is height in meters squared. The formula changes according to the units.
